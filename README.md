## Problem Statement
> **How can households and policymakers predict expected energy cost savings based on household profile — region, income level, energy source, and subsidy status — to make smarter energy investment decisions?**

---

## Dashboard Features
| Visual | Description |

  KPI Cards - Total Savings, Avg Savings, Total & Avg Monthly Usage 
  Map - Savings distribution by Region & Country
  Bar Chart - Total savings breakdown by Energy Source
  Line Chart - Year-over-Year Savings Trend
  Slicers - Region, Income Level, Energy Source, Urban/Rural, Subsidy

---

##  Key Insights
-  Wind energy generates the highest savings — **$59K**
-  Europe & Africa lead in monthly energy consumption
-  Subsidized households save significantly more
-  Middle-income groups have the highest total savings
